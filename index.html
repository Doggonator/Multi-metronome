Welcome to the Multi Metronome, an app designed for conductors and musicians alike to make practicing excerpts with multiple tempos easy. It is a metronome that can be programmed to multiple tempos. To activate the metronome's audio, click anywhere on this page. To advance through your tempos, click the metronome (not the settings menu). To go back, click the left arrow at the left of the screen. To add a new tempo, swap tempos, delete tempos, or clear all tempos, use the buttons in the settings menu. To edit BPMs, use the number input boxes in the settings menu. Buttons also exist to do things like skip back to the first tempo in the order. The settings menu can be minimized using the arrow at its top left. This program is optimized for mobile devices. Note the settings like low power modes may make the metronome inaccurate or inconsistent. Also note that silent modes on some devices can break the audio.
